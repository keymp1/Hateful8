Our Mission

BucStop is a project created by students. The purpose of the website is for ETSU students to be able to play games in their spare time while while waiting for class or on their way to class.

About Us

The creators of BucStop are students at ETSU, consisting of 7 members: Michael Ng, Maxwell Key, Matthew Lockard, Elainna Peay, Ben Smith, Caleb Hammound, and Kincaid Young.
